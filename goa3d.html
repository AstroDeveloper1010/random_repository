Tour Highlights

Duration of Goa 3 days package

The Goa 3-day package is designed for a duration of three days and two nights.

Inclusion of this package

The package typically includes accommodation, transportation, sightseeing tours, and some meals. Exact inclusions may vary depending on your customized package.

Can I customize my Goa 3-day package?

Yes you can customize this package as per your comfort.

Which attractions are covered in the Goa 3-day package?

Popular attractions included in the Goa 3-day package are beaches like Baga, Calangute, and Anjuna, Old Goa churches, Fort Aguada, and local markets. However, the specific attractions may vary depending on the package.
